ABOUT ME 关于我

Improve the quality and equity of education through data-driven empirical research 以数据驱动的实证研究促进教育质量与公平

Hi! I am Xin Liu, an Associate Professor at the College of Teacher Education, Ningbo University (China). My academic journey spans Asia and Europe: I received my PhD in Educational Sciences from Ghent University (Belgium) and a Master of Science in Educational Research from the University of Gothenburg (Sweden). I completed a Postdoctoral Research Fellowship in Educational Psychology at the University of Oslo (Norway). Before moving abroad, I worked as a Lecturer at Daqing Vocational College and earned three bachelor's degrees in China: a Bachelor of Arts, a Bachelor of Education, and a Bachelor of Engineering. 您好！我是刘鑫，宁波大学教师教育学院副教授。我的学术经历横跨亚洲与欧洲：在比利时根特大学获得教育科学博士学位， 在瑞典哥德堡大学获得教育研究理学硕士学位，并在挪威奥斯陆大学完成教育心理学博士后研究。出国前，我曾在中国大庆职业学院任讲师， 并在国内获得文学学士、教育学学士与工学学士学位。

My research specialises in secondary analyses of international large-scale educational assessments (e.g., PISA, TALIS, TIMSS) to understand how student, teacher, and school factors shape learning outcomes (both achievement and affective outcomes), with a particular focus on teaching quality, mathematics education, teacher competence, ICT, well-being, and educational equity across cultural contexts. 我的研究专长于国际大型教育评估（如 PISA、TALIS、TIMSS）的二次数据分析，从跨文化比较视角理解学生、教师与学校因素如何共同影响学习结果 （包括学业成就与情感结果）。研究重点涵盖教学质量、数学教育、教师能力、ICT 相关因素、幸福感，以及教育公平。

Methodologically, I work with quantitative approaches including latent variable modelling, structural equation modelling, multilevel and longitudinal analyses, mediation/moderation, and mixture modelling. 在研究方法上，我主要采用量化研究方法，包括潜变量建模、结构方程模型、多层与纵向分析、中介/调节模型，以及混合模型等。
